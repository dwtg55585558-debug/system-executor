# Product Vision

## One-Line Vision

System Executor is a local-first Trading Cultivation RPG that turns disciplined trading behavior into character growth, training quests, boss resistance, and long-term identity progression.

## Product Promise

The app helps a trader become the kind of person who follows their system. It does this by rewarding process actions, honest journaling, successful waiting, and recovery from behavioral enemies. It must never make profit and loss the source of identity, EXP, or achievement.

## Desired Feeling

The product should feel less like a minimal trading utility and more like entering a dark fantasy cultivation interface:

- Premium and cinematic.
- Mysterious but usable.
- Disciplined rather than chaotic.
- Martial, ritualistic, and focused.
- Built around growth through repetition, restraint, and honest review.

## Visual Direction

Future UI work should follow a dark fantasy cultivation RPG direction.

Core style:

- Black and gold color system.
- Near-black app surfaces with layered ink, charcoal, and ash panels.
- Gold for rank, mastery, progress, completed discipline, and premium emphasis.
- Bronze, ember, jade, crimson, and muted violet as limited supporting accents.
- RPG mobile UI cards with compact hierarchy and strong affordances.
- Cinematic section headers, rank plates, quest cards, equipment/skill slots, boss panels, and achievement hall displays.

Core motifs:

- Character growth system.
- Cultivation rank and identity progression.
- Training quests and daily discipline drills.
- Equipment and skills as metaphors for repeatable trading behaviors.
- Boss challenge system for behavioral enemies.
- Achievement hall for earned discipline milestones.
- Martial arts and cultivation language where it clarifies the experience.

Avoid:

- Generic fintech dashboards.
- Minimal trading-terminal styling as the dominant mood.
- Profit-first scoreboard language.
- Bright SaaS palettes or playful arcade game tropes.
- Excessive ornament that makes daily execution slower.

## Product Pillars

### Identity Before Performance

The user is training to become a System Executor. The app should reinforce who the user is becoming, not whether a trade made money.

### Discipline As Progression

EXP, ranks, achievements, and integrity should be earned by disciplined behavior:

- Morning calibration.
- Planning.
- Pre-trade checklist completion.
- Following the strategy.
- Setting stop loss.
- Successful waiting.
- Reading/training.
- Honest journaling.
- Resisting boss impulses.

### Honesty Over Streak Theater

The app can feel game-like, but it should not encourage fake completion. Warnings, journal gaps, integrity changes, and boss encounters should support truthful self-review.

### RPG Metaphor With Practical Utility

RPG framing should make the app more motivating and easier to scan. It should not hide the actual trading discipline workflow.

## Feature Framing

- Training Hall: daily home base, mission, quote, warning, and training quest checklist.
- Training Grounds: planning, checklist execution, trade logs, waits, violations, and boss resistance.
- Reflection Scroll: daily journal and review.
- Character Sheet: cultivation rank, EXP, integrity, skills, equipment, bosses, and achievements.
- Insight Archive: long-term behavior analytics and discipline patterns.
